## Full Description (4000 chars)

Shipirio is your smart style assistant that digitizes your wardrobe and gives you AI-powered outfit suggestions every day.

🧠 AI OUTFIT SUGGESTIONS
Get personalized outfit ideas based on weather, events, and your mood. Shipirio's style engine analyzes your wardrobe and finds the perfect match.

👗 DIGITAL WARDROBE
Snap your clothes; AI automatically extracts category, color, season, and brand. Your whole closet, always with you.

🛒 ASK BEFORE YOU BUY
Before buying a new piece, Shipirio shows you similar items already in your wardrobe and combination potential. No more wasted purchases.

📊 WARDROBE ANALYSIS
See what you wear most, what's forgotten, and the value of your closet. Smart insights for sustainable consumption.

💰 RESELL RECOMMENDATIONS
For long-unworn pieces, get price estimates and links to second-hand platforms.

⭐ STYLE DNA SCORE
Discover your personal style profile. Minimal, elegant, modern… Learn your style identity and get tailored suggestions.

👥 SOCIAL STYLE SHARING
Share outfits with friends, draw inspiration, and create group looks using items from friends' wardrobes too.

🎯 EVENT PLANNER
Meetings, weddings, dinners — prepare outfits in advance for upcoming events.

📅 OUTFIT DIARY
Log what you wear each day. A 7-day calendar view helps you track style habits.

💬 STYLE ASSISTANT CHAT
Real-time advice from an AI stylist. Ask "what should I wear today?" and get instant suggestions.

🌤️ WEATHER-AWARE
Location-based weather syncs with outfit suggestions. Coat on rainy days, light on warm ones.

💎 PREMIUM FEATURES
• Unlimited outfit analyses
• Daily AI style suggestions
• Pre-purchase compatibility analysis
• Premium wardrobe analytics
• Price tracking and sale alerts
• Priority access to new features

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
GDPR & KVKK compliant. Your data is only used for your personalized suggestions. Delete your account anytime.

Manage your style smarter, love your wardrobe more.
